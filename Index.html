Prophet Muhammad

The hadith of the Prophet, according to the Sunnis and the community, is what was reported from the Prophet Muhammad in terms of sayings, actions, approvals, or descriptions.

Types of Hadith

Authentic_Hadith

It is every hadith that is authentically attributed to the Messenger of God.

More

Weak_Hadith

A hadith that lacks one of the conditions of an acceptable hadith, meaning that it does not have the characteristics of a sound hadith or a good hadith.

More

Suspended_Hadith

It is what is narrated from the Companions of their sayings or actions and the like, so it is stopped at them and does not go beyond it to the Messenger of God.

More

Interrupted_Hadith

It is against the connected hadith, and interruption and connection are characteristics of the chain of transmission.
